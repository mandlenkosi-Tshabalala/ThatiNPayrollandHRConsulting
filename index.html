    About Us

    Why pay more when you can pay less and stress less...

    Are you always paying your employees late? Not sure how to calculate and abide by the Statutory Laws? Are you paying too much just to pay your employees? Let us take away all that stress, all you do is push a button and let us deal with all the stress levels. ThatiN Payroll and HR Consulting provides its services to clients at a lower rate, you only pay for what you get, our rates are based per payslip. We make use of SAGE as our processing system, that allows us to only charge low costs and low bank charges for same day processes. The company is 100% owned by a young ambitious women and will expand with time and try to curb the unemployment rate. It’s outsourced payroll and HR services will make life easy at a very low cost, the more employees you have the less the services costs.




    About the director

    Motswetla Angela Rasehlo Graduated from The University of Johannesburg with a National Diploma in Human Resource Management in 2011. Angela currently has a Sage VIP (Cobalt) and Sage 300 people (SQIL) certificates. She has experience on Premier ,Classic ,Essential and Sage 300 people systems. “I Started working for ICASA in the complaints department as a Data Capture in 2011 for a year and that’s where I learned all the administration work, after my contract ended I worked for a Pharmaceutical company called Nativa as a Switchboard Operator and still felt something was missing. I then got another Job at a security company as a HR administrator, while there I still felt I haven’t found what I’m passionate for until I got an offer at Sage VIP. I was trained by Sage and worked as a Payroll Consultant therefore that alone tells you that I’m the best within the rest…“ “Working for Sage and learning all that is to know about Payroll made me realise the passion and drive I was looking for, and that’s when I decided to make it a reality.“
